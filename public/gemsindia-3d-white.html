Our Story

Sourced With
Integrity



Kikan International Private Limited, trading as GemsIndia, has built one of the world's most extensive collections of natural, large-size collector gemstones. Every stone is ethically sourced, individually inspected, and certified by leading gemological laboratories.

We serve discerning collectors, jewelry designers, and metaphysical practitioners across 50+ countries — from marquise-cut museum pieces to everyday healing crystals.
